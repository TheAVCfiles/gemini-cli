# Syvaq | DeCrypt the Future — Client Delivery Blueprint

## Executive Summary

Syvaq delivers a values-first, trauma-aware founder kit and delivery service. Lilly operates SYVAQ as the living lab; Allison provides DeCrypt the Future guidance, templates, and the astro engine as an evolving research asset. Every engagement is designed for safety, repeatability, and research-grade visibility.

## SOP Timeline

### Onboarding (Day 0)

1. Collect exact founder natal data and entity founding moment (date, time, timezone, location). Confirm consent and release.
2. Create Notion client workspace from template. Build a “safety roster” with three Guardians.

### Research & Vet (Days 1–6)

1. Verify natal times with client.
2. Collect event list, assets, and certification documentation.
3. Run initial pass of astro engine and sanity-check thresholds.

### Prototype Run (Week 2)

1. Produce CSV + calendar; run Cohort 1 (six users) with Guardian monitoring.
2. Capture feedback and annotate anomalies.

### Calibration & Legal (Weeks 3–4)

1. Tune orb/weights; finalize MOU and IP agreements.
2. Document asset exit routes and business continuity safeguards.

### Deliver & Handoff (Weeks 6–12)

1. Finalize deliverables plus 60-minute training.
2. Execute code handoff and two-week post-launch monitoring.

## Roles & Required People

- **Founder (Lilly):** Final decisions, user recruitment, lived proof.
- **Mentor (Allison):** Strategy, choreography, creative narrative.
- **Developer:** Convert Python notebook to production run, Streamlit prototype.
- **Designer:** Sigil, PDF, and landing page assets.

## Minimum Legal / Safety Checklist

1. Signed consent and release for case study participation.
2. Anti-predation clause embedded in MOU.
3. Guardian scripts with escalation pathways.
4. IP clarity outlining ownership and license terms.

## Delivery Variants

- **Sprint Mode:** Two-week intensive for emergent launches (requires pre-vetted guardian roster and abbreviated playbook).
- **Research Partnership:** Twelve-month longitudinal study with quarterly whitepapers and anonymized data share.
- **White-label:** Embed astro engine + guardian protocol into partner platform under joint brand.

## Success Metrics

- Guardian-triggered escalations resolved within 24 hours.
- Client-reported clarity score ≥ 8/10 post-engagement.
- Delivery timeline variance ≤ 10%.
- Astro engine accuracy validated by ≥ 70% correlation with retrospective events.
