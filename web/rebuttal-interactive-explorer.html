2. Risk Gating Protocol: "Cunning Mercy"

Gate A: Volatility Guard

The Volatility Guard (Vol Guard) dynamically measures the 1-hour realized volatility against a 30-day moving average threshold. If the spike is > 2.5 standard deviations, the gate closes and all execution is suspended.

Gate B: Liquidity Check

The Liquidity Check ensures sufficient market depth (min $5M 1-minute volume) to absorb the proposed order size without excessive price impact (slippage). If depth is low, the gate closes to prevent execution risk.
