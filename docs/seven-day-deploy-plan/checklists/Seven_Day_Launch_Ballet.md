# Seven Day Launch Ballet

Use this checklist to land the "Adaptive Ethics" secure edition across the web. Each day builds on the prior moves—check the boxes as you go.

## Day 1 – Anchor the domain and DOI

- [ ] Buy or point a Cloudflare Registrar domain (e.g., `avc.systems`).
- [ ] Create a Cloudflare Pages project and deploy the `/site` directory from this bundle.
- [ ] Upload `Adaptive_Ethics_Vol1_SecureEdition_AvC_2025_DOI-10.5281-zenodo.17282954.pdf` to `/site/assets/` and redeploy.
- [ ] Replace every `YOURDOMAIN.tld` reference in `/site/doi/index.html` and `/content/jsonld_scholarlyarticle.json`.
- [ ] Verify the DOI landing page renders the PDF link and JSON-LD snippet.

## Day 2 – Claim the scholar channels

- [ ] Refresh ORCID with the DOI and abstract.
- [ ] Update Google Scholar with the DOI landing page.
- [ ] Post the abstract to ResearchGate and OSF using `content/abstract_block.txt`.
- [ ] Ensure backlinks to the DOI page are live on each profile.

## Day 3 – Open the compliance funnel

- [ ] Deploy the `/site/scan` form (Typeform, Notion, or custom embed).
- [ ] Set up an auto-reply acknowledging submissions.
- [ ] Add a call-to-action button on the homepage pointing to `/scan/`.

## Day 4 – Publish Temperance

- [ ] Post `content/linkedin_post_1.txt` on LinkedIn.
- [ ] Cross-post the same story to Substack or your chosen newsletter.
- [ ] Capture screenshots for the press kit.

## Day 5 – Share the Balanchine Cipher

- [ ] Publish `content/linkedin_post_2.txt` on LinkedIn.
- [ ] Send a soft-touch note using `/site/press/index.html` to at least two relevant journalists or partners.
- [ ] Archive responses in your CRM or spreadsheet.

## Day 6 – Offer the Mercy Gate

- [ ] Publish `content/linkedin_post_3.txt` on LinkedIn.
- [ ] Send the `/site/echo/` one-pager to three high-priority contacts.
- [ ] Log feedback for next week's iteration.

## Day 7 – Measure and memorialize

- [ ] Pull analytics from Zenodo downloads and Google Scholar citations.
- [ ] Capture screenshots and add them to `/site/press/`.
- [ ] Draft and publish a retrospective blog or LinkedIn article summarizing outcomes.
- [ ] Schedule the next launch sprint.
