# THE ANARETIC EDGE — Lilly’s 29° Protocol — Quick Field Guide
_Date: 2025-11-08_

## Daily
1. **Morning check:** Review composite_score for today + prior 10 days. If any **red** in last 7 days → triage.

## Triage (If red)
2. **Pause:** No launches/signing/irreversible actions.  
3. **Protect:** Activate Guardian check-in (Aura Mode).  
4. **Prepare (24–72 hrs):** Rehearse, document, consult legal & mentor.

## After red clears
5. **Debrief (15 min):** What triggered it? Did Guardian flow work? Update Notion log.

## Weekly
6. **Capacity check (60 min):** with Allison or core mentor.  
7. **Cap mentor hours:** avoid burnout.

### Anaretic Pause Script (60–90s)
