Launch Rhythm

The librarian bundles your checklists, domain tasks, scholar submissions, and narrative assets. Follow the order below and you will move from DOI provenance to first-touch revenue conversations in one week.

  1. Deploy: Publish this site on Cloudflare Pages and drop in the canonical PDF.
  2. Document: Update `/doi/` with your live domain and JSON-LD so Scholar bots listen.
  3. Route: Embed your compliance form at `/scan/` and wire the CTA on this page.
  4. Signal: Release the three LinkedIn posts and circulate the Echo Edition.
  5. Measure: Add screenshots and metrics to `/press/` before closing the sprint.

Immediate Actions

Swap your domain: Search for YOURDOMAIN.tld in this repository and replace it with your live hostname. Update both `/doi/index.html` and `../content/jsonld_scholarlyarticle.json`.

Once your domain is live, import the calendar (`../Seven_Day_Launch_Ballet.ics`) and duplicate the LinkedIn copy from the content directory.
